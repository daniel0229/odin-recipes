Caprese on a Stick

This is a great, easy finger appetizer. I came up with it because I love Caprese but it was difficult to serve at large parties.
Putting the same ingredients on a toothpick yielded great results.

Ingredients

  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 (.6 ounce) package fresh basil leaves
  • 1 (16 ounce) package small fresh mozzarella balls
  • toothpicks
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
